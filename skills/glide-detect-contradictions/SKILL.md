# Glide Detect Contradictions

## Load

- `Glide HQ/AGENTS.md`
- `Glide HQ/Company Context.md`
- `Glide HQ/Marketing Lead Brief.md`
- `Glide HQ/Checklists/Contradiction Review.md`
- `Glide HQ/Contradiction Register.md`
- Relevant areas, decisions, questions, research, and follow-through

## Process

1. Look for tensions between strategy, evidence, behavior, resource allocation, metrics, and constraints.
2. Separate real contradictions from harmless uncertainty.
3. Name why the tension matters.
4. Recommend a review, experiment, question, or decision packet.
5. Update `Contradiction Register.md` when useful.

## Output

- Contradictions or no material contradictions.
- Evidence and impact.
- Suggested next review.
