# Glide Create Marketing Context

## Load

- `Glide HQ/AGENTS.md`
- `Glide HQ/Checklists/Marketing Context Setup.md`
- `Glide HQ/Source Map.md`
- `Glide HQ/Content Library.md`
- Existing README, website copy, product docs, pitch decks, customer notes, metrics docs, investor updates, and strategy notes when available

## Process

1. Inspect available materials before asking questions.
2. Map allowed sources in `Glide HQ/Source Map.md`.
3. Record reusable existing content in `Glide HQ/Content Library.md`.
4. Draft `Glide HQ/Company Context.md`, `Glide HQ/Marketing Context.md`, and `Glide HQ/Marketing Lead Brief.md`.
5. Capture the current pain, goals, and operating process in `Marketing Lead Brief.md`.
6. Ask what is wrong, missing, uncertain, and strategically important now.
7. Capture exact customer language when available.
8. Mark uncertain claims as uncertain.
9. Keep the first version useful, not exhaustive.

## Output

- Drafted or updated company context.
- Drafted or updated marketing context.
- Drafted or updated marketing lead brief.
- Focused questions for high-impact gaps.
