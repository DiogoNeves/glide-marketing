# Glide Create Decision Packet

## Load

- `Glide HQ/AGENTS.md`
- `Glide HQ/Company Context.md`
- `Glide HQ/Marketing Lead Brief.md`
- `Glide HQ/Checklists/Decision Packet.md`
- Relevant area files, research, decisions, questions, and contradictions

## Process

1. Clarify the decision and why it matters now.
2. Identify options, recommendation, evidence, risks, reversibility, metrics affected, and cross-company effects.
3. Fetch or research missing information when it materially changes the recommendation.
4. Draft the decision packet.
5. Do not execute the decision without approval.

## Output

- Decision packet.
- Recommendation with tradeoffs.
- Approval boundary and review date.
