# Skills

Installed Glide skills.

- `glide-create-marketing-context`: create the initial company, marketing, and operator context.
- `glide-update-marketing-context`: refresh marketing context from durable signal.
- `glide-daily-marketing-check-in`: run a concise daily operating pass.
- `glide-weekly-marketing-review`: review marketing progress and plan the week.
- `glide-follow-through-review`: review promises, waiting threads, and commitments.
- `glide-create-decision-packet`: prepare a decision for approval.
- `glide-run-area-review`: review one marketing area.
- `glide-deep-research-subject`: research a subject deeply.
- `glide-detect-contradictions`: find tensions across context, goals, metrics, and behavior.
- `glide-marketing-drift-review`: keep Glide operations aligned with design principles.
- `glide-create-area`: create a new company area.
