# Skills

Installed Glide utility skills.

- `glide-create-marketing-context`: create the initial source map, content library, company context, marketing context, and operator context.
- `glide-update-marketing-context`: refresh marketing context from durable signal.
- `glide-daily-marketing-check-in`: run a concise daily operating pass.
- `glide-generate-content-from-sources`: draft marketing content from existing content and source material.
- `glide-deep-research-subject`: research a marketing subject deeply.
- `glide-create-marketing-skill`: research a repeatable marketing process and encode it as a new skill.
- `glide-follow-through-review`: review promises, waiting threads, and commitments.
- `glide-detect-contradictions`: find tensions across context, goals, metrics, and behavior.
- `glide-marketing-drift-review`: keep Glide operations aligned with design principles.
