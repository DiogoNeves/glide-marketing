# Area Review

Purpose: review one company area.

## Process

1. Load `Company Context.md`, `Marketing Context.md`, `Marketing Lead Brief.md`, and the area files.
2. Identify the area's current objective, context, decisions, questions, and follow-through.
3. Check cross-company dependencies.
4. Find risks, contradictions, missing evidence, and next moves.
5. Recommend small updates to the area files.

## Output

- What matters.
- Risks or contradictions.
- Missing context.
- Suggested next move.
- Files to update.
