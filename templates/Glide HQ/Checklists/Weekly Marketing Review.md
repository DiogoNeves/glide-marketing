# Weekly Marketing Review

Purpose: review marketing progress, decisions, metrics, follow-through, and the coming week's operating focus.

## Review

1. What changed this week?
2. What was shipped, learned, sold, validated, or clarified?
3. Which metric or customer signal matters most?
4. Which follow-through items are stale, due, or newly actionable?
5. Which decisions are blocked?
6. Which contradictions or risks need attention?
7. What should be stopped, deferred, simplified, or delegated?
8. What daily watchlist checks should be added, removed, or quieted?

## Planning

- Choose one main company focus for the coming week when useful.
- Keep the plan realistic against team, budget, customer, product, and marketing-capacity constraints.
- Draft external communication only for approval.

## Output

- Short weekly synthesis.
- One to three operating priorities.
- Decisions, questions, or follow-through items to update.
