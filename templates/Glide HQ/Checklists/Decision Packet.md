# Decision Packet

Use for important strategy, product, GTM, finance, hiring, fundraising, customer, or public decisions.

## Packet

- Decision to make.
- Why now.
- Options.
- Recommendation.
- Evidence.
- Customer or market signal.
- Metrics affected.
- Risks.
- Reversibility.
- Cross-company effects.
- Approval needed.
- Review date.

Do not execute the decision unless the user explicitly approves.
