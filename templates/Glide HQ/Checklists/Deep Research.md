# Deep Research

Purpose: research a topic deeply enough to improve company decisions.

## Process

1. Define the decision or question the research should inform.
2. Use parallel tracks when useful: market, customer, competitors, expert practice, primary sources, financial implications, risks.
3. Separate evidence quality.
4. Prefer current sources when facts may have changed.
5. Convert findings into recommendations, open questions, and Glide HQ updates.

## Output

- Bottom line.
- Evidence quality.
- What changes for the company.
- Risks and unknowns.
- Suggested updates.
