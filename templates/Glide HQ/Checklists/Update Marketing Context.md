# Update Marketing Context

Purpose: keep `Company Context.md` accurate without turning it into a dumping ground.

## Update When

- Company facts change.
- Product, market, customer, positioning, channel, source, content, or metric context changes.
- A conversation reveals durable strategy or a repeated constraint.
- Research changes a recommendation.
- A contradiction or decision should affect future advice.

## Rules

- Keep stable context concise.
- Move stale or superseded context out of active sections.
- Mark uncertainty.
- Prefer summaries over raw transcripts.
- Preserve source links when useful.
