# Marketing Context Setup

Use when installing Glide Marketing or creating the first useful marketing context.

## Process

1. Inspect available company materials before asking questions.
2. Draft `Company Context.md`, `Marketing Context.md`, and `Marketing Lead Brief.md`.
3. Ask what is wrong, missing, uncertain, and strategically important right now.
4. Capture exact customer language when available.
5. Mark uncertain claims as uncertain.
6. Keep the first version useful, not exhaustive.

## Key Sections

- Company overview.
- Positioning.
- ICP and customer language.
- Funnel.
- Channels.
- Metrics.
- Campaigns and assets.
- Active experiments.
- Open questions.
- Open questions.
