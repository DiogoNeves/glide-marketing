# Follow-Through Review

Purpose: prevent important promises, waiting threads, and commitments from disappearing.

## Review

1. Read `Follow-Through Ledger.md`.
2. Check configured sources when useful: email, calendar, CRM, project tools, GitHub, docs, support, analytics.
3. Identify stale, due, high-stakes, or newly actionable items.
4. Decide whether each surfaced item needs a next check, owner clarification, draft, decision, closure, or approval.
5. Suggest the next touch, draft, or decision.
6. Ask before external action, including posting marketing material.
7. Update status, next check, next action, and learning.

## Output

- Stale or due items.
- Suggested next step.
- Drafts needing approval.
- Items to close, simplify, or keep watching.
