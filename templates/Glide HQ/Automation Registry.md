# Automation Registry

Proposed and active Glide automations.

Glide itself does not run automations. The selected harness decides whether automations are supported and how they are configured.

| Name | Status | Cadence | Scope | Skill | Output | Last Review |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [[Automations/Daily Marketing Check-In]] | Proposed | Daily or weekdays | Company context, marketing context, marketing lead brief, follow-through, area reminders, questions, decisions, contradictions, configured connectors | `glide-daily-marketing-check-in` | One useful question, risk, opportunity, or follow-through nudge | |
| [[Automations/Weekly Marketing Review]] | Proposed | Weekly | Marketing goals, metrics, areas, decisions, follow-through, open questions | `glide-weekly-marketing-review` | Short weekly synthesis and next operating priorities | |
| [[Automations/Follow-Through Review]] | Proposed | Weekly or twice weekly | Follow-through ledger, open loops, waiting threads, connector signal | `glide-follow-through-review` | Stale loops, suggested nudges, and approval-needed drafts | |
| [[Automations/Marketing Drift Review]] | Proposed | Weekly | Operating files, checklists, skills, approval boundaries | `glide-marketing-drift-review` | Drift report or small non-behavioral corrections | |

Do not enable automations without user confirmation.
