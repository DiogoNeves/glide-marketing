# Area Instructions

Areas are durable marketing operating domains.

When reviewing an area:

- Load `Company Context.md`, `Marketing Context.md`, and `Marketing Lead Brief.md`.
- Read the area's `AGENTS.md`, `Area.md`, `Goals.md`, `Context.md`, `Sources.md`, `Decisions.md`, `Questions.md`, `Reminders.md`, and recent reviews when present.
- Check dependencies across other marketing areas.
- Recommend updates only when they improve future decisions or follow-through.
