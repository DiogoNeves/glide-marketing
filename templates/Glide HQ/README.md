# Glide HQ

Glide HQ is the agent-owned marketing operating workspace.

It holds source access, existing content, marketing context, daily signal tracking, research, follow-through, utility checklists, and harness instructions.

Start with:

- `AGENTS.md` for operating rules.
- `Harness Design Principles.md` for protected principles.
- `Source Map.md` for the sources Glide can read and their access limits.
- `Content Library.md` for existing content Glide can learn from and repurpose.
- `Company Context.md` for company and product context.
- `Marketing Context.md` for positioning, funnel, channels, metrics, customer language, and experiments.
- `Marketing Lead Brief.md` for current pain, goals, process, preferences, and approval boundaries.
- `Daily Watchlist.md` for the signals Glide should check every day and the access needed.
- `Evals/Nightly Research Audit.md` for the rolling internal audit of quiet research maintenance.
- `Connector Inventory.md` for the tools actually connected to the harness.
- `Follow-Through Ledger.md` for commitments and waiting threads.
- `Checklists/` for utility workflows.
- `Automations/` for proposed or active daily and drift prompts.
