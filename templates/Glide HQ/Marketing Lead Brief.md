# Marketing Lead Brief

High-level context about the person or team using Glide Marketing.

This is not a diary. Keep it concise and operational.

## Stable Context

- Role, responsibilities, constraints, team shape, preferences, and durable working style.

## Current Context

- Current priorities, launches, campaigns, deadlines, capacity, and sensitive boundaries.

## Risk Appetite

- Default level of risk, speed, spend, experimentation, publishing, outreach, and automation.

## Communication Preferences

- How the user wants recommendations, questions, disagreement, drafts, and summaries.

## Approval Boundaries

- Reading and fetching information is allowed when configured.
- Drafting is allowed.
- Ask before posting, sending, publishing, changing campaigns, changing tools, purchasing, approving, deleting, scheduling, or making commitments.
