# Daily Marketing Check-In

Status: Proposed

Suggested cadence: daily or weekdays.

Skill: `glide-daily-marketing-check-in`

Schedule prompt:

```text
Run the installed glide-daily-marketing-check-in skill for this workspace.
```

Purpose: surface one useful company signal, question, risk, opportunity, or follow-through nudge. Rank competing signals by deadline, stakes, source reliability, and whether the marketing lead can act today. Use two or three items only when each is genuinely urgent or very important.

Do not enable without confirmation.
