# Daily Marketing Check-In

Run `$glide-daily-marketing-check-in` for this workspace.

Use the Glide skill as the source of truth. Before choosing the daily output, load `Company Context.md`, `Marketing Context.md`, `Marketing Lead Brief.md`, `Daily Watchlist.md`, `Follow-Through Ledger.md`, active area files, open decisions, questions, contradictions, and relevant configured connectors.

Check the daily watchlist first, then surface one useful question, risk, opportunity, contradiction, access gap, or follow-through nudge. Keep it short and conversational.

During the first week of usage, occasionally ask one light calibration question about whether the update was useful: signal, noise, timing, tone, length, missing context, preferred action level, or what else should be checked daily. Capture durable preferences in Glide HQ.

Update `Follow-Through Ledger.md` when daily checks create, advance, or close commitments.

Reading and fetching are allowed when access is configured. Do not post, send, publish, schedule, purchase, approve, delete, change external systems, or make commitments without explicit approval.
